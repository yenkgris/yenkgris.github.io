How You Can Help

Details on how you can help.

Pamphlets

We have designed a Pamphlet that you youreslf can download, print and place in streetlights, authorized store fronts and anywhere that wont be catalogued as vandalism

Marching

Every 22th of each month, we take over the streets of the city and peacefully protest.

Anyone can join as long as you keep it peaceful.

Parades

In collaboration with many charities and NGO's, we at SeakHelp organize parades every 6-8 months with an earth theme to them

These parades have Marching Bands courtesy of the National Academy of Music, parade floats from the Institute of Arts.

We, of course, don't use any type of motorized vehicles (our floats are electric) or fireworks or any other contaminant

Donations

Since we are an independent organizations, your donations are what keep us afloat

Remember then to only donate if you can, partaking in our protests and marches is enough.
